AWETrim computational workflow

A quasi-steady, multi-fidelity framework for soft-kite Airborne Wind Energy Systems. Click any block to see what it does, the figures it produces, and links to the code and papers behind it.

Each block is clickable. Selecting one updates the panel on the right with a description, a figure, and links to the relevant repository and publication.

Scientific Publications

The methods behind the framework are described in:

  • Computational aerodynamics for soft-wing kite design Poland, Masure, Cayon & Schmehl (2026) · Wind Energy Science (preprint, in open discussion) · the VSM aerodynamic method in detail and the 2D machine-learning airfoil model
  • Translational dynamics of bridled kites: a reduced-order model in the course reference frame Cayon, van Deursen & Schmehl (2026) · Wind Energy Science · shared kinematics & reduced-order model
  • Wind tunnel load measurements of a leading-edge inflatable kite rigid-scale model Poland, van Spronsen, Gaunaa & Schmehl (2026) · Wind Energy Science · wind-tunnel validation of the VSM aerodynamic model
  • Aerodynamic analysis of a two-dimensional rigid leading edge inflatable airfoil van Lith, Poland, Watson & Schmehl (2026) · J. Phys. Conf. Ser. 3224 · wind-tunnel validation of the 2D regression airfoil model
  • Optimal reel-out trajectories for soft kites under varying wind conditions Cayon & Schmehl (2026) · Torque conference · trajectory optimisation, outputs & applications
  • Kite as a sensor: wind and state estimation in tethered flying systems Cayon, Watson & Schmehl (2025) · Wind Energy Science · EKF reconstruction & wind estimation
  • Fast aero-structural model of a leading-edge inflatable kite Cayon, Gaunaa & Schmehl (2023) · Energies · aero-structural model

Code & repositories

AWETrim ties together several open-source packages:

  • AWETrim This framework — CasADi system model, ROM & trajectory optimisation
  • Vortex Step Method (VSM) Enhanced lifting-line aerodynamic solver for low-aspect-ratio wings
  • Particle System Simulator (PSS) Particle-spring structural solver for flexible membrane kites
  • kite_fem Finite-element structural solver, alternative to PSS in the coupling
  • EKF-AWE Extended Kalman Filter for AWE flight-data reconstruction
  • TU Delft LEI-V3 kite Reference kite data, geometry and field measurements
  • awesIO I/O helpers for AWE data formats used by the scripts
  • CasADi Symbolic computation and NLP solving (IPOPT)

Datasets

Open flight-test data sets used as experimental inputs:

  • Flight test · 9 Oct 2025 TU Delft V3 (25 m² LEI), Ireland · most recent V3 flight · kinematics, airflow, ground-station, control inputs & lidar wind
  • Flight test · 5 Jun 2024 Kitepower V9 (60 m² LEI), Bangor Erris, Ireland · kinematics, tether force & angle, reel-out speed, lidar wind
  • Flight test · 27 Nov 2023 Kitepower V9, Bangor Erris, Ireland · IMU/GPS kinematics, pitot & vane airflow, tether force, lidar wind (66 cycles)
  • Flight test · 12 May 2023 Kitepower V9 (60 m² LEI), Dirksland, Netherlands · kinematics, tether force, airflow, ground wind (8 cycles)
  • Flight test · 8 Oct 2019 TU Delft V3 (25 m² LEI), Valkenburg, Netherlands · kinematics, wind, tether force, reel speed & power (87 cycles, 10 Hz)
